About/關於

Telos is a subscription based voting platform for Hong Kong residents to vote on renovation and construction projects, or any other building-/complex-wide projects for that matter, as determined by the appointed chairman and owner's committee. Telos是一個基於訂閱的投票平台，供香港居民投票通過翻修和建設項目，或由任命的主席和業主委員會決定的任何其他建築/綜合性項目。

Telos enables residents to take matters into their own hands, save valuable time, and make informed decisions prior to any project. Telos使居民能夠自己掌握事情，節省寶貴的時間，並在任何項目之前做出明智的決定。

We have also made sure to encrypt your votes to ensure complete anonymity. We are here to make sure you know what you are voting for, and what you are paying for. 我們還確保加密您的投票，以確保完全匿名。 我們在這裡確保你知道你在投什麼，以及你付出的代價。
